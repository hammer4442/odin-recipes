Mushroom Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 1/2 TSP Kosher Salt
  • 1 LBS Wide Egg Noodle
  • 8 TBSP Unsalted Butter
  • 1 LBS Sliced Cremini Mushrooms
  • 1/4 TSP Coarsely Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Minced Large Shallot
  • 3 TBSP All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 1/2 CUPS Vegtable Broth
  • 1 CUP White Wine
  • 1/2 CUP Sour Cream
  • Parsley As Needed

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook the noodles and set them aside
  2. Melt the butter in a large skillet
  3. Add mushrooms, salt and pepper
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes allowing the mushrooms to brown
  5. Stir and cook for anohter 2-3 minutes
